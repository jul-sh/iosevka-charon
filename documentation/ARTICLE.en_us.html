Iosevka Charon is a custom cut of the Iosevka superfamily tuned for coding and terminal work. The quasi-proportional main family keeps dense alignment while adding gentle curves and a wider palette of distinction for lookalike glyphs.

The companion Iosevka Charon Mono preserves the same stylistic decisions in a strict monospaced grid for tools that expect fixed-width metrics. Both families are generated from private build plans on top of the upstream Iosevka sources and are post-processed to meet Google Fonts QA expectations.

Source and build scripts live at github.com/jul-sh/iosevka-charon-gf.
